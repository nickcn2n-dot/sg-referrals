Editor’s note

What this page is trying to do

Most referral pages either throw every promo into one heap or give you too little context to judge whether the reward is actually worth the friction. This page aims for the opposite: fewer offers, clearer notes, and a better sense of what you are clicking into.

Before you click

Three checks that matter

  • Is the offer for new users only?
  • Does it require a deposit, top-up, or holding period?
  • Is the reward actually cash, or is it a coupon / credit / conditional bonus?

How we keep this useful

Small list, clearer judgment

This site is supposed to stay small. If an offer becomes stale, messy, or hard to verify, it should be removed instead of being kept around just to make the page look fuller.

We keep the shortlist intentionally small

The point is not to list every promo in Singapore. The point is to keep the page readable and the recommendations explainable.

We point back to the actual source

Every serious listing should link back to the provider’s campaign terms, support page, or official explanation so you can verify the details yourself.

We avoid fake urgency

No fake countdowns, no made-up “last chance” banners, and no pretending a provider will never change their campaign wording.
