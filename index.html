How we keep this useful

Small list by design

The point is not to list every promo in Singapore. The point is to keep recommendations readable and explainable.

Source-linked summaries

Every serious listing should point back to the provider's campaign terms, support page, or official explanation.

No fake urgency

No fake countdowns, no made-up "last chance" banners, and no pretending a provider cannot change campaign wording.

Remove stale offers

If an offer becomes stale, messy, or hard to verify, it should be removed instead of kept around to make the page look fuller.

How this shortlist is judged

  • Is the reward easy to verify from official provider material?
  • How much user effort is needed: sign-up only, top-up, deposit, transfer-in, or holding period?
  • Is the reward cash-like, coupon-like, or variable?
  • Would the product still make sense if the referral reward disappeared tomorrow?
