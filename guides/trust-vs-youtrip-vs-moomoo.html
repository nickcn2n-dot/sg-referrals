Comparison guide

Trust vs YouTrip vs Moomoo SG

If you do not care about the brand names yet, start here. These three offers serve very different user types even though they all sit under the same referral umbrella.

Last checked: 2026-04-19
Fast read: Trust is the easiest starter, YouTrip is the most practical everyday pick, and Moomoo is only worth the extra work if you already wanted a brokerage account.

Quick answer

Choose Trust if you want the easiest path

It has the lowest explanation burden and the most mainstream product shape. Good default if someone just wants one clean referral option.

Choose YouTrip if you want practical use

The reward is smaller, but the product makes sense for users who travel, spend in foreign currencies, or already like wallet-style products.

Choose Moomoo only if you already wanted a brokerage account

The bigger headline comes with the heaviest stack of conditions. Deposit thresholds, transfer-in rules, coupon mechanics, and holding periods matter more here than on the other two.

What matters more than the headline

1. Friction beats headline reward

A bigger number is not automatically a better referral if it needs deposits, transfer-ins, or long holding periods that users will resent or forget.

2. Product fit matters

The best referral offer is usually attached to a product the user would not regret keeping. That makes Trust and YouTrip easier to justify for broad audiences than Moomoo.

3. Official terms win

If the provider changes campaign wording, limits, or deadlines, the official page overrides this summary immediately.
